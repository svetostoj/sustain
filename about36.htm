Habitat Partners-Home Sustainability Assessments

Sumury

Improving energy efficiency around the home delivers a range of benefits. It can help reduce pressure on household budgets from increasing energy prices, it can improve the level of comfort in our homes and it reduces personal carbon emissions and our own household¡¯s impact on climate change

Our Service

Habitat Partners is a quality, independent sustainability assessment service dedicated to helping home owners and small business improve their utility (electricity, water and gas) use including the thermal comfort of their environments.

Our Clients

We help all sorts of people:
• Families who have higher power bills and want better energy efficiency
• People who are looking at renovating a part or the whole of their homes
• People who are planning to build a new home
• Anyone wanting improved thermal comfort in their homes
• Families looking at renewable energy solutions for their homes


Green Start program

a) provide Australian households with assistance, in the form of a home energy assessment, that will allow them to contribute to Australia¡¯s greenhouse gas emission reduction targets by improving energy efficiency in their homes; and

b) encourage Australian households to engage in activities that will raise both their awareness of the need for, and willingness to participate in, action to address climate change.

What is the difference between a home sustainability assessment and a home energy assessment?

Home energy assessments will still focus on home sustainability but the focus of home assessments under the program will be on energy. This will ensure that the community is provided with the best quality outcomes
